I have been hesitant to finish backing up “The Pink Bin” because I feel that it may not be complete (or else it is overly comprehensive and needs to be sheered of some of its clutter. (Arguably, that entire collection is clutter)).

I feel “obliged” to back up every item which is currently a part of “The Pink Bin” (including this note) because that entire collection seems sufficiently comprehensive to capture the experience of being mired in sexist myopia and “low quality consciousness” in general. The purpose of preserving such a record is to provide myself a tangible reference point in my personal evolutionary timeline about where I was borderline suicidally depressed (and maybe even homicidal when pressed into a corner by bullies).

“Get over yourself,” is what the bullies interject the most. The bullies refer to many kinds of humans (or human like beings) who seem very impatient with me for how I seem so unencumbered, healthy, inspired, and free without their meddling. I get the sense that my bullies are obsessed with sabotaging my visionary endeavors so that I give up and settle for being a prostitute, silent abuse victim, slave, scapegoat, and token crazy person for others to experiment on. It’s like they want the young and the ignorant to think that I am just a crash test dummy who deserves to be physically and mentally assaulted so that other people (the more important people) are spared such torment. I am not okay with forcing any “sufficiently sentient creature” into being a sacrificial object to pay for other people’s conveniences.

As a closing remark, it made me feel good to see one of those “baby on board” stickers on the back window of a car which had that stereotypical picture of a baby wearing sunglasses and, rather than have text above it saying “Baby On Board” it said something like “Financial Burden On Board”. Thank you for being honest! Thank you for showing me you don’t worship the brat you shat out and decided to make everyone pay taxes for.

I have a few more remarks to write down before I close this note. I noticed that I do feel better driving less than once per month. It is possible to get around without a car. It just takes more time, more physical exertion on my part, and more exposure to experiences I cannot control. I do prefer to be outside and “part of the world” than sealed inside of a container designed to keep me preoccupied with my family of origin.

Also, as I was walking, I thought about how my primary website (Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com) looks like it is currently in the midst of some psychological transformation from being dogmatically technocratic and vaguely dystopian (and daresay misanthropic (especially towards “breeders”) towards wanting to be less riddled with baggage and incoherent verbiage. I was looking through stuff I have written thinking that it might indeed be due for a major renovation. I was thinking of deleting all the pages and starting over and making that the main project I focus on this summer: getting my manifestos up to date first and then filling in the cracks with programming examples.

I have been afraid to remove content because I spent so much time already trying to build up some kind of resume/portfolio to showcase so that people can see that I have some college-level professionalism and that I am not just a preacher. I am even more afraid that if I delete content from that website, I will lose part of what enables me to not feel so cornered and force-fed trashy propaganda and anti-inspiration from those who want to discourage me from pursuing my highest goals.

Okay. I think I will leave every page of KO which I have already published and apparently finalized as is. The DISCLAIMER page is designed to put an end to my self-destructive habit of redoing the same content over and over again because I want what I do to be as perfect as possible. Like Moore’s Law approaching a limit about how many transistors can be crammed onto one chip of a fixed size, there seems to be very little wiggle room left for “improving” the content which is already there. I might as well focus on making newer fresher content with better verbiage (i.e. more precise use of vocabulary terms, more succinct sentences, and less metaphors).

I have an idea: suppose I just start drafting a new version of Karlina Object as plain HTML files, code files, image files, and sound files and put all that in a new repository on GitHub which is designed to rekindle my enthusiasm for TECHNOLOGY and which I felt prior to 2016 (especially while I was working on Pumpkin Programmer, working as a paid JavaScript developer at Lawrence Berkeley Lab, and hanging out with an exceptionally bright and futuristic technology expert named Spencer Eugene Dyer).

No offense, but Alan Jason Ponte is a bit too preachy for my taste. I would rather be “cold, white, and robot-like” than “social justice warrior-like”. I do not want my website to be about preaching how I think humans ought to behave. Instead, I would rather focus on being objective, radically accepting of reality, extremely open minded, and unabashedly enthusiastic about science and technology (not as an ambassador but as a developer and researcher and pioneer who goes on quest to explore what I find interesting rather than “looks good on a resume”).

Aha! I got an idea: I will take all the content which is currently encapsulated by Karlina Object dot WordPress dot Com and “engulf it” inside of Karbytes For Life dot WordPress dot Com as a special directory page called KARLINA_OBJECT_OLD_EDITION.

There. Now I will go about my business as smoothly, nonviolently, and fairly as possible. See you on the other side!

Perhaps each lifetime is experienced exactly one time by a particular person (or by sentience in general). Perhaps there are lifetimes yet to be experienced by any person. Perhaps all imaginable lifetimes have already been experienced and are currently being experienced by some “higher” level of reality.

One more thing: forget not that the effeminate bullies who I imagine look like pouting prostitutes are always complaining because they do not really want to be satisfied. They have been conditioned to assume that being happy means that they will be evicted and beaten up. So they have to show off how unhappy they are to keep getting provisions and protections.
