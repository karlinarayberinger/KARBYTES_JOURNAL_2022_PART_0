While I was traveling through the Fruitvale BART station area, I saw the following message inscribed in black graffiti on a BART train track pillar near some dilapidated-looking trailers: “You’re going to need a jump one day…” I can only guess what the artist of that graffiti meant. I interpreted it as meaning that, sooner or later, I (and perhaps every human interested in seriously expanding their consciousness) is going to need some kind of psychedelic to transcend the “ordinary” kinds of cognition which keeps people prisoners of dysfunctional customs (due to fear of the consequences of leaving “the matrix”). Oakland, California (where that graffiti was “planted”) is, to my knowledge, one of the only places in the United States of America which has made psibocilin legal to purchase, posses, and consume.

I remember watching cannabis become more widely accepted and appreciated for its health and recreational benefits since 2015. I watched a local medical marijuana dispensary named Garden of Eden in Hayward, California go from selling mostly only dried cannabis flowers to selling a plethora of other cannabis products. I watched the legal system change from making marijuana something a person would need a doctor’s recommendation letter to purchase to becoming a recreational substance, like beer, which anyone aged 21 years and older could legally purchase, consume, and posses.

Based on the historical trends I have seen in etheogenic plants becoming more widely accepted as having medicinal value and revolutionary potential to change society for the better, I hope and predict that, within about ten years, drug laws in general will become more lax.

I feel personally feel very privileged to be born and raised where and how I was. My parents grow marijuana plants in their backyard and their houses are very close to the Chabot Regional Park wilderness. I am immensely grateful to live so close to that wilderness. As weird as this sounds, I prefer to sleep outside under the stars most nights because it makes me feel like a tiny creature nestled in some gigantic dream scape under a vast and deep sky full of stars, galaxies, and probably aliens. When I look up at the starry night sky, I sometimes see actual Unidentified Flying Objects which might be military drones but could possibly be piloted by extraterrestrials. I assume that aliens are stealthily observing humans. In fact, I remember being sedated and visited by a white alien with big black eyes with brown irises when I was about seven years old. Though it could have simply been a dream or a coverup for humans molesting me, it may have been as literal as it appeared to me and as I recall: an intelligent being from an extraterrestrial origin observing me as a specimen and perhaps setting me up to be some kind of inter-species communication expert.

I know my musings tend to get grandiose when I spend enough time luxuriating in serene spaces, indulging in good food and medicine, and consuming thought-provoking media. When I feel deprived of such goodies, I tend to get very shrill, myopic, overly serious, fearful that I will spiral downward into worsening circumstances, and misanthropic. That’s usually when I start barking about how breeders take up all my space, time, and energy by shitting out too many humans and making everyone’s standard of living worse as a result of overpopulation and widespread indifference to environmental sustainability issues. I get especially irate over environmental issues and the politics involved. That is why I have a goal of driving that petroleum powered van which my parents let me borrow no more than one day per month. I would much rather travel by walking, biking, and riding public transportation (not just for environmental sustainability but so that I can travel more freely without having to metaphorically lug around a burdensome and high-maintenance ball and chain).

I am almost always resentful of the fact that seemingly 95% of all the humans in my midst are not as adamant as I am about switching to 100% sustainable infrastructure and making that upgrade happen as quickly as possible. That is why I am very selective about whom I choose to associate with. Presently, I have almost zero friends and the only people I talk to are those I meet when I go about my business in public or whom I live with. I can tell that many strangers are not very fond of me. It seems like they can literally hear my thoughts even if I don’t speak nor write. How is that possible? How come they seem to be able to hear me but I seem unable to hear them? Why am I being treated like “the bad guy” while everyone else is treated as innocent? I think the government is not particularly in a rush to make environmental justice a major priority. Instead, the legal system seems designed to keep people focused on their families and tending their little households and sneering at those who do not think that is the only legitimate way to be human. (Because I prefer to spend almost all my time alone and have no desire in baring children or adopting orphans or pets, other humans chastise me and call me an overgrown child. I think they are mad that I have not fallen into the same kinds of mind-narrowing, time-sucking, laziness-mongering, ecological indifference traps those people were conned into settling for and those people want me to suffer because they are suffering and they implicit;y equate suffering with being paid to exist).

I’m going to need a jump right now to get me out of this downward spiral train of thought. No matter how good of a message I think I have to share with other people, the people are going to ignore it at best and, at worse, try to censor me for attempting to express my ideas if those people are not already open minded enough to be receptive to ideas they do not already cling to as part of their doctrines about how reality is structured and about how people ought to behave within that limiting structure. I enjoy writing because it verbatim preserves my words in a relatively immortal and public format. I am not a fan of merely imparting my knowledge onto a select few organic human brains because human brains tend to distort information and to not retain information as time elapses. I trust the Internet to do a better job retaining and interpreting my ideas than humans in general. Hence, I am not a teacher standing in front of a classroom full of attention deficit students. Hence, I am not chanting some slogan while marching around in the streets waving a picket sign with my slogan inscribed on it.

It is not my job to make the people feel comfortable. There is no absolute cure to all of humanity’s problems that I know of. There is always uncertainty. There is always the potential for systems to break or to malfunction. There is always the potential for progress and memories to get erased or distorted. That does not mean I will do what many other humans seem to implicitly demand that I do: shut up, give up on trying to make my ideas known to the world outside my head, and go through the motions of earning my keep according to their dictates. What I will do instead is keep journaling like my life depends on it, like I deserve to make it one of my main hobbies, and to keep re-focusing my mind on what I want when I get deterred no matter how many times I get deterred; no matter how futile my attempts to reach my goals may seem to myself and others.

I promote the idea that every person is unconditionally alone inside their own solipsistic reality bubbles (whether or not multiple solipsistic reality bubbles exist concurrently and maybe even interact with each other). I need to mind my own reality bubble in case my bubble casts a ripple effect onto other reality bubbles if my goal is to be as conscientious towards other sentient creatures and not just towards myself. Also, I do not like inhabiting a pigsty. I want my reality bubble to be clean and inspiring and not littered with junk.

What about other people negatively affecting my reality bubble despite my efforts to not negatively affect their reality bubbles? It’s like having to keep pump water out of a boat which keeps getting filled up with water as I attempt to drain that water such that my efforts do little more than keep me from drowning because other people cruelly or recklessly douse me in too much of their waste water. Of course it’s not fair! Cruelty and suffering exist. Sometimes people work very hard to do what they think is morally correct only to be transgressed against. I am trying not to take it too personally. It helps to remember examples of how harsh and cruel some survival mechanisms are in certain animal species. That helps me feel like I have a much better deal than most animals on Planet Earth do because I am an apex predator and a member of the planet’s most intelligent and powerful species: the humans. Though many humans are what I find to be ugly, arrogant pricks, I will strive not to be one of them. Not all humans are ogres I “secretly” would choose to obliterate with a laser which would instantaneously vaporize them such that only those I find worthy of sharing the planet with me remain. (While many of those ogres would call me an evil psychopath who needs to be locked up in a cage and tortured for what I just admitted, I bet those same people would admit something similar to what I said if they were subject to the right kinds of experiences and manipulations. In other words, I bet that everyone is a closeted genocide advocate and would always justify saving their little tribe at the expense of all other tribes).

Here’s something I doubt many family obsessed simpletons would get around to concluding: perhaps the most noble way to prevent genocide and murder is suicide. If there is only enough space on the planet for X amount of people but there is more than X people, some of those people are inevitably going to be pushed off a cliff. How many would voluntarily jump off that cliff so that others do not have to be shoved off the cliff against their will? I think I would be among the first to voluntarily jump. You know why? I want to show other people that they do not have to be assholes to each other. Also, I would not want to have to spend the rest of my life fighting to keep what little I have while the growing families take, take, take from me and threaten to push me off a cliff so that their spawn can take up the space I would be filling. It makes me want to round up the pregnant people first and have them all obliterated first. Before y’all make more people when there is already not enough room to go around for those who are currently alive, you should feel morally obliged to take up as little space as possible (even if that means killing yourself). That is why I have lamented in the past that most children are born because their parents wanted to ensure that they would not be deprived of a decent standard of living (while using those children as “get out of jail free” cards and disposing of those children as soon as they reach 18 years of age). The fact that parents do not seriously make a priority of saving the environment for future generations (even their own lineage) makes me think that most parents are not fit to be parents. That is why I wrote TECHNOCRACY (especially the part about making each new human lab grown, parentless, and produced only at a rate which is sustainable and sufficiently desired by the existing human population. In other words, in my ideal society, no child would be an oopsy poopsy. There would be no need for abortions. All humans would not be allowed to sexually reproduce because they would be born without gametes and the surveillance system would send out miniature abortion-drug-administering drones to stealthily cancel each pregnancy in humans who have gametes. Even better, my ideal society would not let gamete humans join that society. It would say, “Go back to your overpopulated, shit strewn ghettos. This place is not for breeders. Clean up your own mess at home. We do not tolerate your kind here.” I think that kind of libertarian technocratic society is fair. The only people who live there are non-breeders. Those who want to breed can start a colony elsewhere. My utopia will not waste its time and resources taking care of incompetent outsiders. That is not to be mean. That is because my society is calibrated to precisely make use of every calorie and millisecond without wasting a single one. It is a relatively closed system; a self sustaining system; an autotroph of a civilization. My ideal society would have a strict zero tolerance policy for pollution. There would be zero toxic waste. It would not allow such waste to accumulate because even trace amounts are carcinogenic).
