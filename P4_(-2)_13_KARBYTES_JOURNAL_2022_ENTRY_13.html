KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2022_ENTRY_13

Although I have known this fact for a long time, it has taken me this long to finally gather up the courage to just do what I know I should: only eat as much as I know I actually need and stick to a plant-based diet while not being preachy about it (and just go about my business quietly (i.e. without talking out loud to myself or whoever I imagine is some invisible paparazzi following me around with a video camera and microphone recording every little thing I think, say, and do and intensively hanging onto my every word such that every word I speak is radiating with painfully awkward and insincerely hyperbolic charismatic fervor)).

If I did as I “believe” I should, then I will likely sooner rather than later “come down” from being so “high strung” and not cause the thought police and “civilian cops” to want to swarm, smother, scold, and subdue me as much. In other words, I know that it is physically impossible for me to stick to my convictions while, at the same time, continue the loud, extroverted charade I have just because it become an addicting and compulsive habit which I “invested” a lot of time, energy, money, and other coveted resources into constructing and maintaining (apparently for almost no one’s benefit).

Fine. I will just “admit” that I wasted hella time and just stop trying to be so…unique…self important…overly serious…condescending to those around me. I cannot help but think certain unpleasant thoughts about other people when in their midst and I am pretty sure many of those people (if not all of them) can hear the words I am thinking because everyone except for me is already “in the know” about the big technocratic new world order. I am one of the last (if not the last) human beings to finally “get with the program” (but I am not “supposed to” talk about this because I am “supposed to” pretend to be ignorant so that other people can play expert and educate me while I pretend to be surprised and interested and in agreement with what they say (to the extent of what I think such people prefer)). In other words, I will do my best to be a sycophant rather than waste my time and risk getting hurt as a consequence of being too articulate when talking to humans face to face. Not every human being seems to want me to be as honest as I could be (and if I do not appease such people, there will be more fighting and costly messes to clean up). So at the risk of being called a sycophant sell out wimp poser coward loser traitor, I will use more discretion (especially with my words) in the future (god permitting).

No matter what I do to ingratiate myself to other humans, it seems that I can never shrink my ego small enough without killing myself or ending up in bedridden.

Well, perhaps I can find a “happy medium”; a compromise which makes “everyone” happy (or as close to satisfying all being at all times as extensively as possible).

I am “sorry” for being such a perfectionist. I am sorry for overcompensating for being a perfectionist and becoming a slacker. I am sorry for whatever wrongdoings you believe I am responsible for. Sure, I will take the blame just to get the show on the road, but I will not shoot myself in the foot and pretend to be too inept to continue doing what i believe is in everyone’s best interest: doing every person as little harm as possible by isolating myself as much as possible and imposing on other people’s free will as little as possible (without killing myself and without putting myself in a situation which feels like torture).

I think the people will like me more 90 days from now after I consistently prove that I can keep to myself, not bother anyone, not be obnoxiously loud or off time or off key, not loiter too much in crowded public spaces, not talk to myself out loud, keep up to date on news, show that I am actually doing science and math homework and not just talking about doing it eventually, and never again complaining about other people’s conduct (or the state of the environment). I will avoid talking about the news and politics because I am not at all qualified to discuss human affairs. I am just a narcissistic isolationist spoiled special snowflake health obsessed eco Nazi bimbo who needs to stop calling attention to itself and accept that society does not necessarily conform to my ideals.

Rather than merely think about such matters and have them evaporate soon after, I decided to write these thoughts down to back up in my web journal so that I can keep my memories from fading and in an orderly format. That will help me not repeat my past mistakes and show other people that I am attempting to improve my conduct.

I will do my best to be laissez faire and to not tell other people what to do (unless they ask for my advice). Even if people ask for my advice, I might not always give it because I do not trust all people to interpret my advice well.
