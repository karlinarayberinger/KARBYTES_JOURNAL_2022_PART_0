It seems that, as soon as I do anything other than be preoccupied with doing some kind of physically or mentally demanding task such as sit to hear myself think, sniveling snarky thought police start intruding my mind space with harassing words and sound effects which seemed designed to keep me from relaxing and thinking outside the box of what those thought police want me to stay confined inside of. The thought police seem to be justifying what they are doing as not allowing me to procrastinate and to keep me looking perpetually up tight, on guard, depressed, angry, and preoccupied with politics and humanities (while natural science and analytical philosophy is treated as an elitist, and therefore criminal, self indulgent activity which must be discouraged so that people who are less privileged than I am get sadistic pleasure out of holding me back so that they can exploit me as a token Nazi to reduce to an unnaturally simplistic simpleton straw-man whom they censor into only saying and doing what they want me to so that they don’t have to address my real thoughts and actions; so that they can superimpose a scapegoat on top of the real me and attack it because, in their minds, that somehow amounts to social justice).

I imagine that the sniveling snarky thought police have a mascot which looks like an angry young black woman who almost never smiles (and when she does, she barely turns her lips upward in a sarcastic, smug smirk). Her eyes are big and sad looking and so is her voice, but I can tell she is boiling with pent up rage. She makes her voice and breathing sound soft, wimpy, shrill, whiny, slow, and tepid. She complains about how I am not posing correctly, using the right words, and doing anything other than sleeping. I think that, if it were up to her, I would only be awake for about four hour per day and spend the rest of the 24 hours with my eyes shut either sleeping or pretending to be asleep while I listen to her drown out my thoughts so that only her words get to take residence in my mind. To her, such a parasitic takeover of my mind is criminal justice being served; her fighting the Nazis; her getting revenge on people who seemed to enjoy more privileges than her so that now the tables are turned and she gets to be the perpetrator while I get to be the victim (because that is how black and white and anthropocentrically she spins the narrative of all reality). Because she has the power to physically overpower me and essentially rape me, she uses the naturalistic fallacy to justify that “might makes right” and that whatever happens was meant to happen by divine ordinance.

Not long ago I was reading a Wired article about black women physicists who were complaining that they felt marginalized in their careers and treated as inferior to white men. Honestly, I did not see anyone doing anything unfair except for those pouty black women who complained that they were not being taken seriously when they insisted on showing up to work wearing skinny jeans with holes in the knees and having lots of hair. If they work in a laboratory, they, like everyone else, would be expected to keep their hair a manageable length and wear proper safety attire. Rather than focus on their love of physics, a lot of them said that their enthusiasm for the subject was dampened because they were not given special accolades just for being black and female while being professional physicists.

I do not want to spend too much time pontificating about that article and the issues it describes because it seems like a no win situation. Because I am not a black woman, I apparently can never understand the plight of those disillusioned black female physicists and “should” shut up and nod along like a good whipped white person is “supposed to” in order to not be ostracized and deprived of employment, housing, education, and even the right to do anything other than live as a beggar on my knees and always having to ask someone “superior” to me for permission to do almost anything. Apparently, I do not have enough friends who satisfy some arbitrary and implicit diversity quotient requirement to be “qualified” to voice my opinion about anything political. Apparently, I have not gone through enough hazing rituals to earn the right to be taken seriously and to have my ideas heard rather than to be censored because social justice warriors decided to put me on an enemy watch-list and treat me like a wayward child who needs to be more closely monitored than everyone else and treated with more coldness, cruelty, indifference, and hostility than everyone else.

A lot of what I write (including this journal entry especially) could be construed as social justice mumbo jumbo; the quaint and misguided ramblings of an out of touch, inexperienced, spoiled, insecure, shy, and overly sensitive white person; a rainbow special snowflake who has yet to come down from its pompous ivory tower to mingle with the commoners using their standard vernacular and customs.

I do apologize if I offend anyone. I am not deliberately trying to make anyone feel bad. It just seems that, no matter how hard I try not to upset other people, I end up pissing them off anyway. Apparently, I have yet to learn how to play by their rules to their satisfaction and that is why they treat me as being unworthy of receiving donations and page views. Apparently, I am not enough of a world renowned humanitarian and I am too much of a narcissistic bimbo. Maybe I should just stop trying so hard to make other people value me and what I endorse and assume that I am almost completely alone in my endeavors because my endeavors are about generating intrinsic self worth and being extremely self reliant to the point that I make friendships seem unnecessary (and that is a cardinal sin amongst those who treat interpersonal relationships as a prerequisite being healthy, happy, and comfortable).

“Well, if none of your former friends want to talk to you (i.e. they ignore your attempts to reach out to them and tell you to go away), it is probably because you are a shitty person who does not deserve their attention and care,” seems to be what the thought police endorse. Okay. But as I attempt to go about my business being mostly hermetic, nerdy, and as non intrusive towards other people as possible, I get attacked by the thought police for not ingratiating myself to other people. It’s like it is a crime to have no friends and to be happily asocial. It’s like no one is allowed to be happily asocial because, if they were, they just might not need to buy so many things, spend so much time seeking approval via social media and in-person meetings, and hastily rush into romantic relationships and child bearing which corners people into having insufficient funds and insufficient time and insufficient energy to do anything but struggle to support a family (because saying that you struggle to make ends meet to support your family means you are not being a selfish bum like me).

This journal is a bit rushed. It is not really what I enjoy writing about. It is not even all that factually based. It is mostly about my internal conspiracy theories and “private” experience of being molested by thought police while other people seem indifferent or else siding with the thought police. I bet this journal will be used as evidence that I need to be treated as a mental ward outpatient; someone who is not quite mentally stable and well adjusted enough to be taken seriously. So be it. I wrote this journal to make myself feel better after feeling sad because I have almost no friends and I am not very motivated to go out of my way to make friends because I see humans as generally being impatient with me, much more unkind than I am (and my preference to be kind is seen as childish and quaint), indifferent to my futuristic ideals, preachy, jealous, fickle, and basically more like undercover cops and supervisors than peers of mine.

One thing I have been experiencing lately that I have not before as a result of taking shrooms is the sense that I deserve to seriously fight for my right to exist and to go after what I desire without having to ask other people for permission to do so. If I did, I might end up waiting a very long time and waste a lot of my precious time, energy, and opportunities to be happy as a result. As blasphemous as this sounds, the themes of my journaling seems to be radical self advocacy and the right to be alone; the right to assert the idea that just because something is widely popular does not mean that thing is actually healthy or validated by science.

Thank you for your patience and understanding as I gradually, day by day, make progress towards understanding reality. My goal is to go deeper as an introvert while at the same time go deeper in terms of being outwardly focused. There is no “correct” balance between inward and outward focus (though society seems to impose some kind of ratio about what is appropriate and legal). I think that it is best to control things as minimally as possible and error on the side of being “too isolationist” and “too introverted” rather than error on the side of being “too socialist” and “too extroverted”. There. I hope I am done dwelling on these macabre, banal, non scientific subjects.

I hope that, starting tomorrow, I can commit to not wasting any more time beating myself up (and others) over not earning the approval of those who seem to be more socially mainstream and higher up on the totem pole than I am. While they accuse me of being too self focused and having nothing interesting to talk about, I hope that I can dismiss them as being hypocritical pests who are really not interesting in doing anything other than keep me from garnering self esteem and satisfaction from my experiences and accomplishments.

One last thing: some people seem to think that people like me (and perhaps people in general) need to be encumbered with human-made obstacles because people would be too happy and therefore too complacent otherwise. I think that real life offers plenty of real challenges to keep humans perpetually preoccupied with solving problems by coming up with innovative solutions because, as a person’s worldview becomes more sophisticated, the kinds of problems that worldview can address become more complex as well. I do not think human beings have to go out of their way to create more suffering and difficulty than there already is unless they are doing so solely in the context of a sandbox universe computer game simulation. Real world challenges seem to arise naturally. I think a lot of the news focuses on problems that have implementable solutions yet, to keep the people busy, the people are fed narratives about how war is inevitable and how we cannot afford to do real science and engineering because, right now, such things are expensive and time consuming luxuries which take the focus off being embroiled in addicting political battles which are primarily the result of grossly lopsided economic distribution, ecological neglect, and widespread propaganda which keeps the masses focused on being in perpetual crisis while they wait for some rich visionary like Elon Musk to wave a magic wand and solve all the world’s problems even while “the peasants” incessantly and hypocritically complain that he is not doing enough while such people do little more than go to menial jobs which robots ought to be doing by now. I rarely see blue collar people talk about automation and universal basic income as a viable and radical way to revolutionize the global economy and raise every person’s standard of living. I guess such people are not really given much opportunity nor incentive to think outside the box of poor, embittered, exploited union worker who can do little more than join their fellow union members in going on strike; refusing to work until they get a pay raise (as if that will seriously improve economic and environmental conditions globally and not just for their myopic little circle of concern).
